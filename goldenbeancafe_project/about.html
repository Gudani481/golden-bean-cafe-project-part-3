About Us

Founded in 2018, Golden Bean Café began as a small neighborhood coffee shop with a mission: to bring the finest artisan coffee and homemade treats to the Melville community. We carefully source our beans from sustainable farms and roast them in-house to ensure freshness in every cup.

Our team is passionate about crafting coffee experiences that go beyond the ordinary — whether it's a classic Flat White or a seasonal special, every drink is made with love and expertise. We also take pride in our locally baked pastries, made fresh daily by our talented bakers.

More than just a café, we strive to be a community hub. From hosting live music and art shows to coffee tasting workshops, Golden Bean Café is a place where locals gather, connect, and celebrate the simple joys of life.
